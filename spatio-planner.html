How It Works

Getting started with Spatio Planner is simple. In just four steps, the AI takes over the heavy lifting of scheduling — so your day runs itself.

  1. 1
    📋

    Add Tasks & Deadlines

    Enter your tasks, set deadlines, and assign priority levels. The more detail you provide, the smarter your schedule becomes.

  2. 2
    📌

    Mark Fixed or Movable

    Decide which tasks are locked in place — like meetings — and which the AI can freely reschedule to find the optimal slot.

  3. 3
    🤖

    AI Optimizes Your Day

    The AI engine analyzes priorities, time blocks, and habits to generate a streamlined, realistic daily timeline automatically.

  4. 4
    ✅

    Track & Complete

    Mark tasks done, track habit streaks, and review your timeline at a glance. Stay accountable and celebrate your progress.

Getting Started

How to Use Spatio Planner

No tutorials needed. Spatio Planner is built to be intuitive from the very first session. Here's all it takes to get going.

  1. 1

    Add tasks with deadlines and priority levels

    Enter work deliverables, personal errands, recurring habits. Set a due date and flag what's most urgent.

  2. 2

    Assign fixed or movable status to each task

    Pin time-sensitive commitments like calls or meetings. Mark everything else as movable so AI finds the best slot.

  3. 3

    Let AI generate your optimized schedule

    Hit optimize and watch your tasks arrange into a logical, efficient daily timeline — automatically.

  4. 4

    Complete tasks and track your habit streaks

    Check off tasks as you go. The habit tracker records your consistency and rewards you with streaks.

  5. 5

    Review your daily timeline anytime

    Glance at your visual timeline to stay on track. Reschedule with a quick drag if your day shifts unexpectedly.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with Spatio Planner.

Is Spatio Planner free?+
Yes. Spatio Planner is completely free and open-source. There is no cost to get started and no hidden pricing tiers.
What devices does Spatio Planner support?+
Spatio Planner works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices. The responsive layout adapts to any screen size automatically.
Does Spatio Planner work offline?+
Yes. Spatio Planner uses local storage so it works fully offline — no internet connection or server required once loaded.
How does the AI scheduling work?+
You add tasks with deadlines and priority levels, mark which are fixed (like meetings) or movable, and the AI engine analyzes your priorities, time blocks, and habits to generate an optimized daily timeline automatically.
Does it sync with Google Calendar or Notion?+
Yes. Spatio Planner connects with Google Calendar and Notion so your entire workflow lives in one place without duplication or manual copying.
When is the app launching?+
The alpha version is estimated to launch on 15/06/2026. You can contact us at leonhexunxu@gmail.com to be notified when it's live, or follow progress on GitHub.
